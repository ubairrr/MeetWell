Purpose: TMPL-01/TMPL-02 are not satisfied by a UI control alone — the selected type must actually persist on the `meetings` row for the meeting that gets created. Output: A `meetingType` parameter on `CaptureService.startCapture()` and a validated, propagated `pendingMeetingType` in `src/main/index.ts` that reaches every new meeting's DB row.
</objective>
