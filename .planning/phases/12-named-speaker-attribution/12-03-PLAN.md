<objective>
Wire the main-process IPC surface for named speaker attribution: a roster query the rename modal fetches on open, and the batch rename-and-propagate call the modal invokes on Save. Both handlers enforce the `Complete`-state gate server-side and validate their payload with Zod before touching any store, matching every existing handler's Zod-validate-then-store-write shape in this file.

Purpose: SPKR-01 requires renaming to be available "during or after a meeting" but CONTEXT.md's D-04 scopes it to post-meeting only for this phase — the FSM gate is the server-side enforcement of that scope decision, not merely a UI convenience. SPKR-05's cross-meeting isolation is only as strong as the Zod-validated meetingId that reaches SpeakerAliasStore.
Output: Two new `ipcMain.handle` registrations in `src/main/index.ts`, two new preload allowlist entries.
</objective>
