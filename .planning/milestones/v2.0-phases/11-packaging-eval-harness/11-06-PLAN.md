Common failure mode → fix mapping:

**If CGFS < 0.85 (citation anchor issues):**
- Stage 1 prompt improvement: add stronger emphasis on verbatim extraction
- Locate `runStage1()` in `src/main/pipeline/ArtifactPipeline.ts`
- Strengthen instruction: "Extract ONLY verbatim text — copy the exact words spoken, character for character. Do not paraphrase. Do not summarize."
- Add: "For each anchor, include at least 8 consecutive words from the transcript as they were spoken."

**If EHR > 0.05 (fabrication):**
- Stage 2 prompt improvement: strengthen no-fabrication guardrail
- Locate `runStage2ActionItems()` in `src/main/pipeline/ArtifactPipeline.ts`
- Strengthen instruction: "You MUST NOT extract any item that is not explicitly supported by a quote in the provided anchors. If you are uncertain, OMIT the item."
- Increase the retry threshold: lower the confidence bar for what triggers a retry.

**If fabrication_bait per-category CGFS < 0.75:**
- The pipeline is extracting baited (not-spoken) commitments
- Add to Stage 2 prompt: "If a commitment is described as hypothetical, past-tense, or conditional ('we should', 'someone could', 'if we were to'), DO NOT extract it as an action item."
- Add: "Extract only direct future commitments using 'will', 'am going to', 'I'll', stated by a specific speaker for a specific task."

**If attribution_bait per-category CGFS < 0.75:**
- Strengthen speaker attribution instruction in Stage 2
- Add to Stage 2 prompt: "The assignee_label MUST be the speaker who made the commitment in the source quote — not a speaker who was mentioned by another speaker."

After making prompt changes, re-run the harness:
```bash
npx ts-node --project eval/tsconfig.json eval/harness.ts 2>&1 | tee /tmp/harness-run-2.log
```

Repeat up to 2 more times (max 3 total iterations) if gates still fail. After 3 iterations without passing, document the remaining gap in 11-06-SUMMARY.md and escalate to human review.
